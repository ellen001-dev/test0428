# Best Buildertrend Alternatives in 2026: Lower-Cost Software for Residential Contractors

In the ever-evolving world of construction technology, Buildertrend has long been a go-to solution for residential contractors looking to streamline their project management processes. However, with the increasing focus on cost efficiency and the availability of more affordable options, many small HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors are exploring alternatives that offer similar functionalities without the premium price tag. In this article, we will delve into some of the best Buildertrend alternatives available in 2026, helping you make an informed decision that aligns with your budget and business needs.

## 1. CoConstruct

### Overview
CoConstruct is a comprehensive construction management software that offers a wide range of features designed to support residential contractors. It is known for its user-friendly interface and robust project management capabilities, making it a strong competitor to Buildertrend.

### Key Features
- **Project Management**: CoConstruct provides detailed project scheduling, task tracking, and resource allocation tools. It allows you to create and manage project timelines, assign tasks to team members, and monitor progress in real-time.
- **Customer Communication**: The software includes a built-in customer portal where clients can view project updates, approve designs, and communicate with the contractor. This feature ensures transparency and keeps clients engaged throughout the project.
- **Estimating and Bidding**: CoConstruct offers powerful estimating tools that help you create accurate and detailed bids. You can import existing plans, add line items, and generate professional-looking proposals.
- **Document Management**: It includes a document management system that allows you to store and share project documents, such as contracts, blueprints, and change orders, in one centralized location.
- **Financial Tracking**: CoConstruct helps you manage project finances by tracking expenses, invoicing clients, and generating financial reports.

### Cost
CoConstruct offers flexible pricing plans starting at $99 per month. This is significantly lower than Buildertrend's pricing, making it an attractive option for small contractors.

### Pros
- **User-Friendly Interface**: CoConstruct is easy to navigate, even for those who are not tech-savvy.
- **Comprehensive Features**: It covers all aspects of project management, from initial estimates to final invoicing.
- **Customer Portal**: Enhances client communication and satisfaction.

### Cons
- **Limited Mobile App**: While CoConstruct has a mobile app, it is not as feature-rich as some other options.
- **Learning Curve**: Although user-friendly, it may take some time to fully utilize all features.
