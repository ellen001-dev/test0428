# Workiz Pricing Review: Is It Worth It for a 3-Man Crew? (2026)

Workiz has emerged as a popular choice among small to medium-sized service businesses. But with its tiered pricing structure, many contractors wonder if it's truly worth the investment for a growing 3-man crew. Let's dive into what Workiz actually costs and whether it delivers enough value.

## Pricing Tiers

Workiz offers three main pricing tiers:

### Basic ($99/month)
- 1 user
- Basic scheduling
- Invoicing and payments
- Customer management

### Pro ($199/month)
- Up to 5 users
- Advanced scheduling
- Route optimization
- QuickBooks integration

### Premium ($299/month)
- Unlimited users
- Custom reporting
- API access
- Priority support

## Value for a 3-Man Crew

For a 3-man crew, the Pro tier seems to be the sweet spot. At $199/month, you get all the essential features without overpaying for enterprise-level tools you may not need.

## Conclusion

Workiz offers solid value for growing service businesses. The Pro tier provides excellent features for a 3-man crew at a reasonable price point.
