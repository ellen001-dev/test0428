# Jobber Pricing 2026: A Comprehensive Guide

Jobber is a leading field service management software designed to streamline and optimize the operations of small HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors. With its robust features, Jobber helps businesses manage schedules, invoices, customer relationships, and more, all from a single, intuitive platform. However, one of the most critical aspects of choosing any business management software is understanding its pricing. ## Understanding Jobber's Pricing Structure

Jobber offers a tiered pricing model, which means that the cost of the software increases with the number of users and the complexity of features you need. This approach allows businesses of all sizes to find a plan that fits their unique requirements without overpaying for unnecessary features. As of 2026, Jobber provides several pricing tiers, each tailored to different levels of business activity and user count.

### Jobber Pricing Plans

1. **Starter Plan**
   - **Cost:** $39 per user per month
   - **Features:**
     - Unlimited customers and estimates
     - Basic scheduling and dispatch
     - Invoicing and payment processing
     - Mobile app for field technicians
     - Basic reporting and analytics
   - **Ideal For:** Small businesses with 1-2 users who need essential tools to manage their operations.

2. **Professional Plan**
   - **Cost:** $79 per user per month
   - **Features:**
     - All features from the Starter Plan
     - Advanced scheduling and dispatch
     - Custom branding for invoices and estimates
     - Customer portal with self-service options
     - Enhanced reporting and analytics
     - Project management tools
   - **Ideal For:** Medium-sized businesses with 3-5 users who require more advanced features to scale their operations.

3. **Enterprise Plan**
   - **Cost:** Custom pricing (contact Jobber for a quote)
   - **Features:**
     - All features from the Professional Plan
     - Multi-user access with role-based permissions
     - Advanced project management
     - Integration with third-party tools and systems
     - Dedicated customer support
     - Customizable workflows and integrations
   - **Ideal For:** Large businesses with more than 5 users who need highly customized solutions and dedicated support.

### Additional Costs to Consider

While the base pricing for each plan is straightforward, there are a few additional costs to consider when choosing Jobber:

1. **Setup Fees**
   - Jobber offers a free setup process for all plans. However, if you require extensive customization or integration with other systems, you may need to pay for additional setup services.

2. **Transaction Fees**
   - Jobber charges a transaction fee of 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction for processing payments through their system. This fee is consistent across all plans.

3. **Add-ons**
   - Jobber provides various add-ons that can enhance your experience, such as:
     - **Customer Texting:** $10 per user per month for unlimited text messages to customers.
     - **Advanced Reporting:** $20 per user per month for more in-depth and customizable reports.
     - **Jobber Forms:** $15 per user per month for creating and managing custom forms.
