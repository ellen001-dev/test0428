# 7 Best Procore Alternatives in 2026: Cheaper Options for Small & Mid-Size Contractors

In the dynamic world of construction, project management software has become an indispensable tool for contractors, helping them streamline operations, improve communication, and enhance productivity. However, the high cost of premium solutions like Procore can be a significant barrier for small and mid-size contractors. Fortunately, the market is teeming with affordable alternatives that offer robust features and functionalities. In this article, we will explore the top 7 Procore alternatives for 2026, tailored specifically for small and mid-size HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors. These options are designed to provide the same level of efficiency and functionality at a fraction of the cost, ensuring you get the best value for your investment.

## 1. CoConstruct

### Overview
CoConstruct is a comprehensive construction management software that is particularly well-suited for small and mid-size contractors. It offers a wide range of features, including project management, scheduling, budgeting, and client communication tools. CoConstruct is known for its user-friendly interface and customizable options, making it a popular choice among HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors.

### Key Features
- **Project Management**: CoConstruct provides detailed project planning and tracking tools, allowing you to manage tasks, milestones, and resources efficiently.
- **Scheduling**: The software includes a powerful scheduling module that helps you keep track of project timelines and deadlines.
- **Budgeting**: With CoConstruct, you can create and manage detailed budgets, track expenses, and generate financial reports.
- **Client Communication**: The platform offers built-in communication tools to keep clients informed and engaged throughout the project lifecycle.
- **Document Management**: Store and organize all project documents, including contracts, change orders, and invoices, in one centralized location.

### Pricing
CoConstruct offers flexible pricing plans starting at $49 per month for a single user. This makes it an excellent option for small contractors who need a cost-effective solution. For larger teams, the pricing scales up but remains competitive compared to Procore.

### Ideal For
HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors who need an all-in-one solution for project management, scheduling, and client communication.

## 2. Buildertrend

### Overview
Buildertrend is another top choice for small and mid-size contractors, especially those in the residential construction sector. It focuses on project management, scheduling, and customer communication, providing a seamless experience for both contractors and clients.

### Key Features
- **Project Management**: Buildertrend offers robust project management tools, including task lists, Gantt charts, and real-time project tracking.
- **Scheduling**: The scheduling module helps you plan and manage your project timelines, ensuring that everything stays on track.
- **Customer Communication**: The software includes a client portal where you can share project updates, photos, and documents, keeping clients in the loop.
- **Estimating and Budgeting**: Buildertrend provides tools for creating accurate estimates and managing project budgets.
- **Mobile App**: Access project data and communicate with clients on the go with the Buildertrend mobile app.

### Pricing
Buildertrend offers a variety of pricing plans starting at $25 per month for a single user. For teams, the pricing increases but remains cost-effective, making it a great alternative to Procore.

### Ideal For
Residential HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors who prioritize client communication and project transparency.

## 3. PlanGrid

### Overview
PlanGrid, now part of Autodesk, is a construction management software that specializes in document management and collaboration. It is particularly useful for contractors who need to manage and share large volumes of project documents.

### Key Features
- **Document Management**: PlanGrid allows you to store, view, and share construction documents, including blueprints, specifications, and RFIs, in a centralized cloud-based platform.
- **Collaboration**: The software facilitates real-time collaboration among team members, subcontractors, and clients, ensuring everyone is on the same page.
- **Markups and Redlines**: You can add markups and redlines to documents, making it easy to track changes and updates.
- **Field Reporting**: Generate and share field reports, including daily logs, punch lists, and safety reports.
- **Mobile App**: Access and manage project documents from anywhere with the PlanGrid mobile app.

### Pricing
PlanGrid offers pricing plans starting at $10 per user per month. For small and mid-size contractors, this can be a highly affordable option, especially for those who prioritize document management and collaboration.

### Ideal For
HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors who need a reliable document management and collaboration tool.

## 4. JobNimbus

### Overview
JobNimbus is a CRM and project management software designed specifically for the construction industry. It combines customer relationship management with project management features, making it a versatile tool for contractors.

### Key Features
- **CRM**: JobNimbus helps you manage leads, clients, and contacts, streamlining your sales and marketing efforts.
- **Project Management**: The software offers project tracking, task management, and scheduling tools to keep your projects organized.
- **Estimating and Invoicing**: Create accurate estimates and invoices, and manage payments and financials.
- **Document Management**: Store and share project documents, including contracts, change orders, and invoices.
- **Mobile App**: Access your CRM and project management tools on the go with the JobNimbus mobile app.

### Pricing
JobNimbus offers pricing plans starting at $39 per month for a single user. For teams, the pricing scales up but remains affordable, making it a cost-effective alternative to Procore.

### Ideal For
HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors who need a CRM and project management solution to manage leads and projects effectively.

## 5. Fieldwire

### Overview
Fieldwire is a project management and communication tool designed for construction teams. It focuses on task management and collaboration, making it an ideal choice for small and mid-size contractors who need to keep their teams aligned and productive.

### Key Features
- **Task Management**: Fieldwire provides a simple and intuitive task management system that helps you assign, track, and complete tasks efficiently.
- **Collaboration**: The software includes real-time communication tools, allowing team members to collaborate and share updates.
- **Document Management**: Store and share project documents, including blueprints and specifications, in a centralized location.
- **Field Reporting**: Generate and share field reports, including daily logs, punch lists, and safety reports.
- **Mobile App**: Access and manage tasks and documents from anywhere with the Fieldwire mobile app.

### Pricing
Fieldwire offers a free plan for small teams, making it an excellent option for contractors who are just starting out. For more advanced features, the paid plans start at $8 per user per month.

### Ideal For
HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors who need a task management and collaboration tool to keep their teams organized.

### Key Features

- **Collaboration**: Facilitate real-time collaboration among team members, subcontractors, and clients.

### Ideal For
HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors who need a comprehensive solution with BIM integration and advanced collaboration features.

## 7. Unanet

Unanet is a project management and accounting software designed for the construction industry. It offers a suite of tools to help contractors manage projects, resources, and finances effectively.

### Key Features

- **Resource Management**: The software helps you manage and allocate resources, including labor, equipment, and materials.
- **Accounting and Financials**: Unanet includes advanced accounting and financial management tools, allowing you to track budgets, expenses, and payments.

Unanet offers pricing plans starting at $75 per user per month. While it is slightly more expensive than some other options, the comprehensive suite of tools makes it a valuable investment for contractors who need advanced financial and resource management capabilities.

### Ideal For
HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors who need a project management and accounting solution to manage both projects and finances effectively.
